First Imperial Cargo, Inc. is a wholly Filipino corporation established in April 1986 by Mr. Luis V. Vega. We are a leading logistics provider in the Philippines with over 35 years of experience in freight forwarding, customs brokerage, and comprehensive logistics solutions.

We have grown from a single office into an internationally recognized company with strategic partnerships worldwide, serving clients across the U.S., Canada, Europe, Middle East, and Asia Pacific regions.

First Imperial Cargo, Inc. has strategically located offices across the Philippines:

  • Manila: Our head office and primary operations center
  • Lapu-Lapu City, Cebu: Established in 1992, serving Central Visayas
  • Cavite EPZA, Rosario: Our first branch office established in 1989
  • Davao: Southern Philippines operations center

First Imperial Cargo, Inc. holds several important certifications and qualifications:

  • IATA (International Air Transport Association) qualified agent since 1989
  • Licensed customs brokerage services
  • Authorized freight forwarding operations
  • Bonded warehousing facilities
  • Marine insurance provider certification

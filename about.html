About First Imperial Cargo

Building trust through decades of excellence in global logistics

Our Story

Established in April 1986 by Mr. Luis V. Vega as President and Chairman, First Imperial Cargo, Inc. is a wholly Filipino corporation that has grown from a single office into a leading logistics provider in the Philippines, recognized internationally for its commitment to excellence.

Through hard work, dedication, and a tremendous ability to meet our clients' requirements, we have propelled our company to the forefront of the country's leading domestic and international forwarding industry. What started as a vision to connect Filipino businesses to global markets has evolved into a comprehensive logistics powerhouse.

Our strategic partnerships with worldwide partners from the U.S., Canada, Europe, Middle East, and Asia Pacific regions have forged mutually beneficial relationships, increasing our range of services and efficiency of cargo delivery worldwide.
